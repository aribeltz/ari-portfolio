Projects:

Xplorstem Academy

A web application built around the concept that short-form videos make learning easier and more engaging for kids. Following the scientific process, users make a hypothesis, watch and respond to research videos to grow their knowledge, then complete a hands-on experiment to round out the entire experience.

Pokemon Starter Battle

Originally built as a challenge to create a more engaging version of rock-paper-scissors, this site allows users to choose from 5 generations of pokemon and battle them against a CPU.

WoW Translator

This simple site was built after I realized that there was currently no alternative for users to access the racial text translations that are available in-game. It uses official translation tables to provide a way to speak in-character, outside of the game.
